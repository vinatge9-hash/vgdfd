Our Story

Founded by a group of neighbors who believed coffee is more than a drink — it is the daily ritual that brings people together. At Cozy Cup, we roast with care, source beans ethically, and invite you to slow down and savor the moment.

From the first bean to the last sip, our mission is simple: craft exceptional coffee while building a warm, inclusive space where everyone feels at home.
